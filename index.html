The Process

How the archive remembers.

Three moves, all automated on the Salesforce platform. Nobody re-types a note or keeps a spreadsheet of "maybes."

01 — Capture

Raw notes in, structure out.

Interviewers paste their Slack notes into a scorecard. The AI reads them, files a 1–5 score, and lifts out every skill it can name.

02 — Remember

The runner-up is kept.

When a role closes and someone else is hired, the strong runner-up's file is stamped a Silver Medalist — summary, skills, and scores saved for good.

03 — Rediscover

The file finds its moment.

A new role opens. The archive matches its own records against the brief and hands back a ranked shortlist — each pick with a reason.

Two Clerks

The archive keeps two agents on staff.

One recalls the right person for a new role. The other briefs you before you sit down with them.

Agent 01 · The Recaller

Silver Medalist Recommender

Reads every closed file — the AI interview summary, the extracted skills, the proficiencies — and matches them against a new job's description. It returns a ranked top three, writes the picks onto the opening, and can reach back out to the candidate by email.

Agent 02 · The Briefer

Pre-Final-Round Summary

The moment a candidate reaches the final round, it reads every scorecard across every stage and writes one honest paragraph — the consensus, the strengths interviewers actually named, the concerns raised, and the fit for the role. You walk in briefed, not buried in tabs.

The Holdings

What's on file.

Seven record types hold the full hiring lifecycle. Technical and behavioral rounds can run in either order — the round type on each scorecard drives the logic, not a fixed sequence.

PrefixRecordWhat it holds
JOB-Job OpeningThe role, its description, and the AI-recommended Silver Medalist picks.
CAND-CandidateThe person — contact details, source, and Silver Medalist / Hired flags.
APP-ApplicationLinks a candidate to a job; tracks the stage and the AI progress summary.
SC-ScorecardOne interview round — raw feedback in, AI score and recommendation out.
SKL-SkillAI-extracted skills, tied back to the scorecard that produced them.
SM-Silver MedalistThe permanent memory of a strong runner-up.
OT-Onboarding TaskThe final-round checklist for the selected candidate.
